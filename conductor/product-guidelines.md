# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style & Communication
- **Tone:** Professional and Formal.
- **Principles:**
    - Use clear, unambiguous language suitable for enterprise-grade documentation.
    - Maintain a structured and serious tone that instills confidence in the project's robustness.
    - Avoid slang or overly casual expressions; prioritize precision and professionalism in all communications, including API error messages and logs.

## Visual Identity & Brand Personality
- **Aesthetic:** Modern and Minimalist.
- **Principles:**
    - **Clean Interface:** Leverage whitespace to reduce cognitive load and focus on content.
    - **Typography:** Use clean, sans-serif fonts for readability and a contemporary feel.
    - **Color Palette:** Stick to a sophisticated and sober palette (primarily black, white, and grays) with subtle accents only where necessary to guide user action.
    - **UI Components:** Adhere strictly to Shadcn/UI primitives, ensuring consistency and a polished finish across the entire platform.

## Quality Standards
- **Technical Excellence:** Every feature must be strictly typed and follow the architectural patterns established in the monorepo.
- **AI-Ready Documentation:** All guidelines must be written so they are easily interpretable by both humans and AI agents.
